Name: Tempe Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Tempe Chamber of Commerce purpose is to unite local businesses with the community and other local groups to increase the economy of the city of tempe. Chamber of Commerce sponsored events provide opportunities for local advertising opportunities. Chamber members will have their business featured on the Chamber of Commerce website for the public to view.

  • How to gain sponsorship for my event?
  • How much is membership?
