Non profit

Non Profit Organizations can join for free. This includes being added to The Tempe Chamber of Commerce website. Access to the newsletter and request to have local businesses support your activities and fundraisers.

Bronze Member

This allows for members access to advertising on Our website and activities. New letter and the use of The Tempe Chamber of Commerce on business cards and adverts. Only $600 annually.

Silver Member

Sliver members have access to our shared customer info. Name and logo on all Tempe Chamber of Commerce activities. Link on the website. Chamber meetings and networking events only $ 1200 annually.

Gold Member

Gold member and full access to our directory of businesses contacts voting for activities. Invites to all networking events. Brunch fundraiser. All the perk of the other membership for $3000 annually.
